## "CSE le lo, baaki sab bekaar hai" — sach kya hai?

Aaj kal har koi CSE (Computer Science) ke peeche bhaag raha hai, aur baaki branches ko "scope nahi hai" bolkar khaarij kar diya jaata hai. Ye poora sach nahi hai.

Har branch ka apna scope hai — bas alag industries mein. Galat branch achhe college se bhi tumhe frustrate karegi; sahi branch average college se bhi aage le jaayegi. Chaliye honest dekhte hain.

## CSE — Computer Science

**Reality:** Sabse zyada jobs, sabse acchi starting salary, aur work-from-anywhere flexibility. Software, AI, data, web — sab yahin se.

**Catch:** Competition bhi sabse zyada. Sirf degree kaafi nahi — coding khud se practice karni padti hai. Jo college ke bharose baithe rehte hain, woh CSE mein bhi struggle karte hain.

**Kiske liye:** Jo problem-solving aur logic enjoy karte hain, ghanton screen pe kaam kar sakte hain, aur khud seekhne ki aadat hai.

## ECE — Electronics & Communication

**Reality:** Ek versatile branch. Tum software side (coding jobs ECE walon ko bhi milti hai) ya core electronics (chip design, telecom, embedded systems, IoT) dono mein jaa sakte ho.

**Catch:** Core electronics jobs kam hain par achhe hain; isliye bahut se ECE wale software mein shift ho jaate hain.

**Kiske liye:** Jo hardware aur software dono mein interest rakhte hain, options khule rakhna chahte hain.

## Mechanical — "Evergreen" branch

**Reality:** "Mech mein scope nahi" ek aadha sach hai. Software boom ke saamne dab gaya, lekin manufacturing, automobile, EV, aerospace, aur energy mein real demand hai — especially ab jab India manufacturing push kar raha hai.

**Catch:** Starting salary aksar CSE se kam hoti hai, aur growth dhime shuru hota hai par stable rehta hai.

**Kiske liye:** Jo machines, design, aur physical cheezein banane mein interest rakhte hain. EV aur robotics ka future yahin hai.

## Civil — Infrastructure ki backbone

**Reality:** Jab tak construction hota rahega (sadak, building, metro, bridge) tab tak civil engineers chahiye. Government jobs (PWD, railways, municipal) aur infra companies mein solid demand.

**Catch:** Private sector salary aksar slow start leti hai. Government route (GATE/PSU/SSC JE) is branch ki asli taakat hai.

**Kiske liye:** Jo construction, planning, aur government sector mein interest rakhte hain. Site work se darr nahi lagta.

## Ek important sach

**Branch se zyada tumhari mehnat matter karti hai.** Ek CSE wala jo coding nahi karta, ek Mechanical wale se peeche reh jaayega jo apni field mein skilled hai. Branch ek shuruaat hai, guarantee nahi.

## Decide kaise karein

Trend ke peeche mat bhaago. Ye poochho:

1. **Kaunsi cheez mujhe genuinely interesting lagti hai?** Code? Machines? Circuits? Construction?
2. **Mera plan kya hai — private job, government job, ya higher studies?** (Government chahiye → Mech/Civil + GATE strong hai)
3. **Kya main khud seekhne ko taiyaar hoon?** Har branch mein self-learning hi aage le jaati hai.

Agar genuinely confuse ho aur koi strong interest nahi — CSE/ECE options khule rakhte hain. Lekin agar machines ya construction tumhe excite karti hai, trend ke chakkar mein woh mat chhodo.
